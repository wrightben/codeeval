# Spiral Printing<br />
RackSpace Interview<br />

## Description:

**Input samples:**

> 1 2 3<br />4 5 6<br />7 8 9 

or

>a b c d e<br/>
n o p q f<br/>
m t s r g<br/>
l k j i h

**Output sample:**

> 1 2 3 6 9 8 7 4 5
